Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a venue?

We recommend booking venues at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for popular dates. For larger productions or peak seasons, 6-12 months is advised to ensure availability.

What's included in the venue rental fee?

Base rental includes the venue space, standard lighting and sound systems, and basic janitorial services. Additional services like technical staff, ushers, and specialized equipment can be added for extra costs.

Can I visit the venue before booking?

Yes, we encourage site visits before finalizing your booking. Contact our venue manager to schedule a tour at a convenient time.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before the event receive a 75% refund. 60-89 days: 50% refund. 30-59 days: 25% refund. Less than 30 days: no refund. Force majeure circumstances are handled case-by-case.
